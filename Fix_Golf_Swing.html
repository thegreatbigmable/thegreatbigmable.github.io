Steps to remember from golf lesson
Alec Gabel 7/15/2020

  1. Shoulder
    2.
    • Keep shoulder square and straight
  2. Club Face
    3.
    • Keep club face square to the ball as it was lined up behind the ball. should go square half way up swing.
  3. Left Knee
    4.
    • Left knee should bend straight to ground. It does not fall back towards right leg. Weight is shifting to the back leg.
  4. Hips
    5.
    • Hips turn and exaggerate. Front point of hip does not go back. Weight
  5. Body Motion
    6.
    • Shoulders relaxed and arms follow the hips and motion of the shoulders
